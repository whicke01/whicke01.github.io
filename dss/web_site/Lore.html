The Lore

“there is nothing as bad as a dead squirrel in memory,” said Professor Sheldon. He pauses for a moment then starts off on a tangent, “Well almost nothing. There was that time that we had undead squirrels crawling out of the Halligan basement…”

In 2015, nuclear research conducted by graduate students in the chemistry department and occult experiments initiated by stressed-out Comp40 students triggered a rift in reality that opened a portal to hell in the basement of Halligan Hall. The only way to close the portal and save the world is to delve deep into the madness-inducing labyrinth and slay the Dead Squirrel.
