Repository for Sektor CS: A 2-d game by Louis Ades, Will Hickey, Ryan Schumacher, and Adam Zakaria.

Set in the far future, an alien force has enclosed Earth in a force field and is threatening its destruction! You play the role of a spaceship captain whose goal is to destroy the four generators that are powering this force field. It is your job to navigate space, acquire new weapons, fight alien invaders, and destroy the four generators.
